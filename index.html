THE MISSION

RoboXplore offers an engaging, hands-on introduction to the world of practical robotics, designed to take you from the basics of autonomous systems to the creation of a fully functional machine. Throughout this session, you will dive deep into the synergy between sensors, motors, and microcontrollers, learning exactly how these components communicate to bring a robot to life. Beyond just theory, you will assemble and program your own Line Following Robot, mastering the logic required for a machine to detect and navigate a predefined path automatically.

This workshop is more than a technical lesson; it is an opportunity to develop vital skills in electronics, creative problem-solving, and complex system design. The experience culminates in an adrenaline-pumping Line Following Bot Competition, where you will put your engineering to the test. By fine-tuning your code and hardware, you will compete against fellow participants to prove who can build the fastest, most efficient, and most precise bot in the arena.

COMPETITION FORMAT

The Track

A 3cm wide black line on white flex. Includes acute angles, 90° turns, crossovers, and a curved bridge. Total length approx 15 meters.

Evaluation

Primary metric is Time. Penalties added for checkpoints missed or manual touches (resets). The bot with the lowest aggregate time wins.

Structure

The competition will consist of a single round where each team navigates their bot through the track. The completion time of each bot will be recorded, and the team with the lowest time will be declared the winner.
